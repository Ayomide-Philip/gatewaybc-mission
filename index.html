Our Purpose Statement | Our Mission and Vision as a Church.

  1. The Gateway Baptist Church (Community of Grace) exists to fulfill the Great Commandment and the great Commission.

    2. The following is then Our Mission Statement:

    To bring people to jesus and membership in his family.

    To develop them to Christ like maturity.

    To equip them for ministry in the church and for life mission in the world, and to magnify Gods name as a result.

  2. To organize and maintain the following programmes and departments.

    1. Sunday School;

    2. Church Traning Programme;

    3. Women Missionary Union;

    4. Men Missionary Union;

    5. House Fellowship / Cell Groups;

    6. And any other programmes and departments as may be approved by the Nigerian Baptist Convention.

  3. To encourage group activities for enhancing the growth of the church.

  4. To establish and maintain School(s) where children shall be educated under Christian influence.

  5. To co-operate with the (Emmanuel) Baptist Association, Ibadan, Oyo West Baptist Conference, and the Nigeria Baptist Convention in carrying out their aims and objectives as setout in their respective constitution and bye-laws.

  6. To carry out the financial duties laid down in the "Accounting Regulations and procedure" as may be formulated for the church.

  7. To worship God in Spirit and truth.

  8. To observe the ordinances of Baptism by immersion and the Lord's supper.

  9. To obeserve all other functions as laid down by the Nigerian Baptist Convention.

  10. To do all such other lawful things as are incidental or conducive to the attainment of the above objects.
